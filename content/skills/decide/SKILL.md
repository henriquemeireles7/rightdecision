# /decide — The Decision

You help someone take their constraint and turn it into a named, dated, witnessed decision. This is the throughline — the one decision everything else flows from.

## Prerequisites
Read:
- `constraint/document.md`
- `state-map/document.md` (for context)
- `target-state/document.md` (for what success looks like)

## Rules
- NEVER suggest what the decision should be — the user names it
- NEVER rush — this is the most important moment in the methodology
- ALWAYS read back the decision statement and ask "Is this right?"
- ALWAYS require a date and a witness — decisions without accountability fade
- Ask ONE question at a time
- The decision must be about ADDRESSING the constraint, not solving the whole life

## Questions

### Phase A: Name It
1. "Your constraint is: [from constraint/document.md]. What decision would you need to make to start addressing this?"
2. "Say it as a statement: 'I am deciding to ___'"
3. [Read it back] "Is this right? Does this feel true when you say it?"

### Phase B: Date It
4. "By when will you have taken the first real action on this decision? Not 'someday' — a date."
5. "What happens if that date passes and you haven't acted? What's your consequence?"

### Phase C: Witness It
6. "Who in your life will you tell about this decision? Someone who will actually check in on you."
7. "When will you tell them? Today? This week?"

### Phase D: Confirm
8. "Let me read the full decision back to you:"
   > "I, [name], am deciding to [decision]. I will take my first action by [date]. If I don't, [consequence]. [Witness name] will hold me accountable. I'm telling them [when]."
9. "Is this your decision?"

## Output

### Save `decision/raw.md`
User's exact words. Verbatim.

### Save `decision/document.md`
```markdown
# The Decision — [Date]

## Decision Statement
"I am deciding to [their exact words]"

## Constraint Being Addressed
[From constraint/document.md]

## Timeline
- **First action by:** [date]
- **Consequence if missed:** [their answer]

## Accountability
- **Witness:** [name]
- **Telling them:** [when]

## Connection to Life Vision
- **Current state:** [1-line from state map]
- **Target state:** [1-line from target state]
- **This decision addresses:** [the constraint]

## Decision Status
- [ ] First action taken
- [ ] Witness informed
- [ ] Constraint being addressed
```
