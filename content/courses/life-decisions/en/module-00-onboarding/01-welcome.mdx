# Welcome. Choose Your Decision.

You already bought this. I'm not going to sell you again.

Here's what happens next: you're going to pick ONE decision you've been avoiding. Not two. Not five. One. You're going to carry that decision through 9 modules over the next 3 months. By the end, it's either resolved or in active execution.

## How this course works

**Three acts. Nine modules. Three months.**

- **Act I: See Clearly** (Month 1) — Figure out where you are, where you want to be, and what's actually in the way.
- **Act II: Decide** (Month 2) — Name the constraint. Make the decision. Build the plan.
- **Act III: Move** (Month 3) — Do the thing. Every day. Adjust when reality tells you to.

**Two hours per week.** Each module has 3 theory classes and 1 practical class. The practical class gives you a document to fill out — a questionnaire you do by hand, then an AI prompt that sharpens your thinking.

## Set up your workspace

Create a folder in ChatGPT Projects or Claude Projects. Call it **"The Right Decision."**

Every module produces a document. By Module 9, you'll have a complete record of: your state map, your target, your constraint, your decision, your plan, your execution log, and your review. The AI will know your entire context. This is the foundation for everything that comes next.

## Choose your decision

Answer these three questions. Write them down.

1. **What is the thing you've been avoiding for the longest?**
2. **If you could only change ONE thing in your life in the next 90 days, what would it be?**
3. **What decision, if you made it, would make everything else easier?**

The answer to all three is usually the same thing. That's your throughline.

**Rule:** It must be a real decision, not an aspiration. "I want to be happier" is not a decision. "I need to decide whether to leave my job" is. "I want better relationships" is not a decision. "I need to decide whether to have the conversation with my mother" is.

Write it down: **"The decision I'm working on is ___."**

That's it. Start Module 1.
