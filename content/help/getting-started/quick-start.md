## Create Your Account

1. Go to [therightdecision.com/life](/life)
2. Click "Get Started"
3. Enter your email and create a password

## Start Your First Lesson

After creating your account, you'll be taken to the first lesson. Each lesson takes about 10-15 minutes to complete.

The course is designed to be completed at your own pace. There's no rush.

## Need Help?

If you run into any issues, email us at hello@rightdecision.com. We respond within 24 hours.
