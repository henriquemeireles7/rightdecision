## Before You Start

The hardest part of any decision isn't choosing. It's admitting you're stuck.

If you're reading this, you've already done the hard part. You've acknowledged that something in your life needs to change, and you're ready to do something about it.

## Step 1: Name the Decision

Don't overthink this. Write down the decision you're facing in one sentence:

- "Should I leave my job?"
- "Should I move to a new city?"
- "Should I end this relationship?"

If your sentence has an "and" in it, you have two decisions. Pick one.

## Step 2: Feel the Resistance

Notice what happens in your body when you read that sentence. The tightness in your chest, the knot in your stomach, the voice that says "but what if..."

That resistance is information. It's telling you this decision matters.

## Step 3: Start the Course

The Right Decision course walks you through a structured process for making this decision. It's not therapy. It's not advice. It's a framework that helps you see what you already know.
