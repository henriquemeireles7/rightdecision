# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 13, 2026

The Right Decision LLC ("we", "us", "our") operates rightdecisions.io. This page explains what data we collect, why, and what we do with it.

## What We Collect

When you create an account or make a purchase, we collect:

- **Email address** — to create your account, send purchase confirmations, and communicate with you about the service
- **Name** — to personalize your experience
- **Payment information** — processed securely by Stripe (we never see or store your full card number)

When you visit our website, we automatically collect:

- **Usage data** — pages visited, time on site, referring pages (via PostHog analytics)
- **Device information** — browser type, operating system (for improving the experience)

## Your Playbook and Journal

If you use the Life Playbook, Starter Notebook, or daily journal, you write things there that you may never have told anyone. We treat this as the most sensitive data we hold — more sensitive than your payment details.

- **What it is** — your answers to playbook pages and your morning/evening journal entries
- **Who sees it** — only you, and the AI features that personalize your experience. No employee reads it in the normal course of business. It is never used for marketing, never shared with third parties, and never sold
- **What it's used for** — only to personalize your experience inside the product (for example, helping you break down the decision you wrote about). Nothing else
- **AI processing** — when AI features process your entries, they do so solely to serve you in that moment. *(We will expand this section with specifics — which provider processes what, and what is retained — before the AI features launch.)*
- **Deletion** — deleting your account deletes every playbook answer and journal entry with it, automatically and permanently
- **Export** — you can take it with you: your filled playbook is exportable from within the product, and you can request a full copy of your data at any time

## How We Use Your Data

- **Account management** — creating and maintaining your account
- **Course access** — delivering the course content you purchased
- **Communication** — purchase confirmations, important service updates (we do not send marketing emails unless you opt in)
- **Improvement** — understanding how people use the site to make it better

## Third Parties

We share data with these services, and only as necessary to operate:

| Service | Purpose | What they receive |
|---------|---------|-------------------|
| **Stripe** | Payment processing | Email, name, payment details |
| **PostHog** | Analytics | Anonymous usage data, IP address |
| **Railway** | Hosting | Server logs (IP addresses, request data) |
| **Resend** | Email delivery | Email address, email content |

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share it with advertisers.

## Cookies

We use minimal cookies:

- **Session cookie** — keeps you logged in (essential, cannot be disabled)
- **Analytics cookies** — PostHog (you can opt out via your browser's Do Not Track setting)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** — request a copy of the data we hold about you
- **Delete** — request that we delete your account and associated data
- **Correct** — request correction of inaccurate data
- **Export** — request your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hsameireles@gmail.com.

## Data Retention

- Account data is retained while your account is active
- If you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days
- Payment records are retained as required by law (typically 7 years)

## Children

Our service is not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email.
