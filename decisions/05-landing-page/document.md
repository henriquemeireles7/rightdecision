## 3. Mechanism (insight-first)

### Why nothing has worked until now

**The insight:**
> Every self-help framework you've tried does the same thing: it helps you understand the PAST.

> Therapy processes your trauma. Energy work aligns your chakras. Manifestation visualizes the future. Coaching gives you someone else's diagnosis.

> None of them ask the one question that actually changes your life:

> **"What are you going to DO about it? Specifically? By when?"**

> That question is uncomfortable. Which is exactly why nobody asks it.

**The mechanism (simple version):**
> The Right Decision is built on one idea: **you are stuck because you're not making decisions, not because you lack understanding.**

> The methodology works in three phases:

> **See Clearly.** Map where you actually are (facts, not stories) and where you want to be (conditions, not metrics). Find the ONE thing in the way.

> **Decide.** Name it. Commit to it. Tell someone. Set a date. Not a wish. Not a plan. A decision with a deadline and a witness.

> **Move.** Break the decision into daily actions. Do one thing per day. The AI helps you decompose YOUR goal into YOUR specific decisions based on YOUR starting point.

> The same goal requires completely different decisions for different people. The AI asks questions about YOUR life to find YOUR path.

**CTA:** Start for $197/year →

## 5. Curriculum

### 9 modules. 3 acts. 3 months.

> This is not a course you binge on a Sunday and forget by Wednesday.

> It's a 3-month system. Two hours a week. Each module ends with a practice that produces a real document about YOUR life, YOUR decisions, YOUR plan.

**Act I: See Clearly (Month 1)**

| Module | What you'll do |
|---|---|
| 1. The Wake-Up Call | Understand why "doing the work" keeps you stuck. Name your discomfort. |
| 2. Where You Actually Are | Write your honest state map. Facts, not stories. Numbers, not narratives. |
| 3. Where You Want to Be | Define your target state as a specific Tuesday you'd want to live. |

**Act II: Decide (Month 2)**

| Module | What you'll do |
|---|---|
| 4. The One Thing in the Way | Identify your dominant constraint. The ONE obstacle that matters most. |
| 5. The Decision | Commit. Write it down. Say it out loud. Tell someone. Set a date. |
| 6. The Plan | Build a one-page plan. Objectives, tasks, habits. Starting tomorrow. |

**Act III: Move (Month 3)**

| Module | What you'll do |
|---|---|
| 7. Doing the Thing | One task per day. Not motivation. Just the thing. |
| 8. What Reality Tells You | Weekly 15-minute reviews. Adjust or persist based on real data. |
| 9. Resolution + Next Loop | Close the cycle. Start the next one. Each loop is faster. |

> **By the end:** 9 documents in your AI project folder. A complete record of your decision, your plan, and your results. Proof that you can do this.

### CTA placement map

CTAs appear at:
1. Hero (primary)
2. After Problem section (mid-page link)
3. After Mechanism section
4. After Curriculum section
5. Offer section (primary, with guarantee)
6. Final CTA section

### Stripe integration

- Single product: $197/year recurring
- Checkout: Stripe Checkout (hosted), redirects to /purchase/success
- Webhook: handles checkout.session.completed
- Auth: Better Auth, email + password, magic link flow on success page

### Mobile strategy

**Priority order on mobile (top to bottom):**
1. Hero (headline + CTA only, subheadline truncated)
2. Problem (shortened — 2 paragraphs, not 5)
3. Offer + Guarantee (moved UP on mobile — price + guarantee visible without scrolling past 3 screens)
4. Mechanism (simplified to 3 bullets)
5. Curriculum (accordion per act)
6. Founder story (collapsed by default, expandable)
7. FAQ (accordion)
8. Final CTA

Sections 4 (Transformation), 7 (Social Proof), 9 (Disqualification) are BELOW the fold on mobile. Still accessible but not in the critical first 3 screens.

### Launch vs post-launch

| Section | Launch (Day 1) | Post-launch (100+ customers) |
|---|---|---|
| Social proof | Founder story only | Real testimonials + numbers |
| Hero headline | Test all 4 variants | Use winner |
| Offer | Standard | Add "Join 500+ women who decided" |
| Urgency | None (honest, no fake scarcity) | None (same — product speaks for itself) |

### Page speed targets

- First Contentful Paint: <1.5s
- Largest Contentful Paint: <2.5s
- No hero image (text-only hero loads instantly)
- Fonts: preload Instrument Serif + Sans
- No third-party scripts until below the fold (analytics, Stripe)
