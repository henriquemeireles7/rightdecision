## Q5: The Self-Help Graveyard (Why They Fire Previous Solutions)
> They actualy finished the course/book/program. They loved it. They have done the practice for days, weeks maybe even months. They have the idea that they are improving, that things are improving. But then some event happens in their life and they realise that they aren't where they want to be. So they try again, another practice, another cycle of self-improvement. Then something happens again and they see themselves stuck. So they are inside an eternal self-help loop that never ends while they improve their lives bit by bit, they do a lot when they had the first breakthough then they stop and they go back into their old self, old habbits, old life. The thing is that they kind of fire the previous one when they find the next one - the cycle never ends. That is the problem. They don't transform their lives though decision and action, they try to do the practice instead of doing the work.
