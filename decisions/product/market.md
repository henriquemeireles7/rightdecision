# Product Market Context

> Last verified: 2026-04-09

## ICP: Life Decisions

Women 30-50 who have "done the work" but are stuck. Anti-self-help positioning.
They've bought courses, read books, done therapy — and still can't decide.
The Right Decision is the last thing they buy because it's the first thing that works.

## ICP: Business Decisions

Non-tech entrepreneurs who want to run their own Decision-based course/program.
They don't code. They don't want dashboards. They want AI skills that do the work.
White-label: each client gets their own instance of the platform.

## Competitive Landscape

| Category | Players | Our Differentiation |
|----------|---------|-------------------|
| Self-help courses | Masterclass, Mindvalley, Udemy | Anti-self-help. Action, not content. |
| Decision tools | Decision Matrix apps, pros/cons lists | Methodology-first, not tool-first. AI-powered. |
| Coaching platforms | BetterUp, Coach.me | $197/yr not $200/session. Course + AI, not 1:1 humans. |
| White-label course platforms | Teachable, Kajabi, Thinkific | Not selling a platform — selling a methodology + AI. |

## Pricing

- Free tier: 3-part intro course (funnel)
- Life Decisions: $197/year (or $19.70/month)
- Business Decisions: $1,997/year (Phase 2+)

## Distribution Strategy

Content-led growth: blog, social, Substack. SEO/GEO for decision-making queries.
No paid ads until $50K ARR proves organic demand.
