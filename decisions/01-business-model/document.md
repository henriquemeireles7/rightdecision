## 2. Customer + Problem

### Persona 1 — "The Stuck Achiever" (Life Decisions)

**Who she is:** A woman, 30-50, who has done the work. She moved cities, changed jobs, built a career, tried therapy, read the books, did the courses. She is objectively ahead of her peers.

And she is stuck. Not at the beginning. Stuck at a higher level, where the problems are harder to name and the solutions are harder to find.

**What she actually does today:** She consumes. She buys the $47 course on feminine energy. She reads a self-help book a month. She follows 15 Instagram coaches. She journals every morning. She meditates.

She is committing the third sin of decisions: **disguising indecision as research.** Reading one more book, taking one more course, having one more conversation, not to inform a decision, but to postpone it.

She is also committing the first sin: **solving the second-biggest constraint because the biggest one is scary.** She optimizes her morning routine while the real problem — the relationship or the career or the move — sits untouched.

**What it costs her:** Time (2+ years thinking about the same decision). Energy (unmade decisions drain like background apps). Compounding opportunity cost (every day of delay is a day of the better life she is not living).

**Copy angle:** *"You already know what to do. You just haven't decided."*

### Persona 2 — "The Overthinker" (Life Decisions)

**Who he is:** A man, 25-40. Smart, capable, consumed by analysis. He reads business books, watches YouTube breakdowns, follows 20 thought leaders. He has frameworks for everything and decisions on nothing.

He wants to start a business, change careers, move cities, or make a major life shift. He has the information. He has the plan. He has seventeen versions of the plan. He has not started.

**What he actually does today:** He researches. He builds spreadsheets comparing options. He creates business plans that never get executed. He asks for advice from people who have less context than he does. He calls this "being strategic." It is sophisticated procrastination.

He is committing the fifth sin: **decomposing without deciding.** He has the tasks, the timeline, the Notion board. What he doesn't have is the commitment. The decision is implicit, never explicit, never spoken, never witnessed.

**What it costs him:** Years. He will look back at 40 and realize the gap between his first plan and his first action was not preparation. It was fear wearing a suit.

**Copy angle:** *"You don't have an information problem. You have a decision problem."*

🟡 *Confidence: hypothesis. Henry lived this pattern. Validated informally through male friends.*

### Persona 3 — "The Drowning Builder" (Business Decisions)

**Who they are:** A non-tech entrepreneur, 28-45. They have an idea, a skill, or an early-stage business. They know their domain — fitness, nutrition, coaching, creative work, consulting. They do NOT know how to systematize, distribute, or automate.

They are drowning in planning: which platform, which tool, which funnel, which course about courses. They've spent $2,000-10,000 on business courses that taught theory without giving them a working system.

**What they actually do today:** They post inconsistently on social media. They have a half-built website. They have course content in Google Docs that nobody has seen. They know they need to "just ship it" but they don't know what "it" is. They need someone to give them the exact playbook AND the tools to execute it.

**What it costs them:** Their business stays an expensive hobby. Revenue is zero or inconsistent. They continue trading time for money in their day job while the business they want to build gathers dust in a Notion workspace.

**Copy angle:** *"Stop preparing. Start deciding. We give you the tools to execute."*

### Why existing solutions fail (all three personas)

The entire self-help and infobusiness education industry optimizes for one thing: understanding. Understand yourself. Understand marketing. Understand funnels.

Understanding is an input to decisions, but most people use it as a substitute for decisions. They research endlessly, consume content obsessively, and call it progress. It is not progress. It is sophisticated procrastination.

- **Therapy** ($150-300/session): Processes the past. Does not decide the future.
- **Life coaching** ($200-500/hour): Expensive and creates dependency on the coach's diagnosis. Sin #2.
- **Online courses** ($47-2,997): Teach frameworks without applying to YOUR situation, YOUR constraint, YOUR starting point.
- **Business courses** ($997-4,997): Teach theory without giving working infrastructure. The student still has to figure out the tech.
- **Free AI** (ChatGPT): No methodology, no context memory, no framework for identifying the dominant constraint.

## 3. Solution + Mechanism

### The core thesis

Every meaningful change — in your life or your business — begins with a decision. Not with information. Not with motivation. Not with a plan. A decision.

You are not stuck because you lack resources, time, or luck. You are stuck because you are not making the right decisions, or you are making no decisions at all — which is the worst decision of all.

The main cause behind not making decisions is complexity. AI helps you have clarity.

### The methodology: The Decision Cycle

Both products use the same 7-step methodology:

1. **Honest State Mapping** — Where are you actually? Facts and numbers, not stories.
2. **Target State Definition** — Where do you want to be? Conditions, not metrics.
3. **Dominant Constraint Identification** — What is the ONE thing in the way?
4. **Decision Commitment** — Name it. Commit to it. Tell someone. Set a date.
5. **Decision Decomposition** — Break it into objectives, tasks, and habits.
6. **Execution** — One task per day. Not motivation. Just the thing.
7. **Feedback + Re-evaluation** — Weekly review. Adjust, persist, or backtrack.

The cycle repeats. Life is an infinite evolution path. New constraints always come. Each pass is faster because you've done it before.

### Product A: Life Decisions ($197/year)

**What it is:** A course that teaches the decision methodology + AI-powered Claude skills that guide each exercise.

**Delivery mechanism:**
- **Course:** 3 acts (See Clearly, Decide, Move), 9 modules, ~23.5 hours over 3 months at 2h/week. Pure methodology. Exercises are AI skill interactions — thinking-first, structuring-second.
- **Skills:** One Claude skill per methodology step. User installs skills in Claude Cowork. Each skill asks deep questions, saves raw answers, and generates a structured document. Same meta → input → document pattern used throughout the Right Decision methodology.
- **AI intro class:** One class in onboarding teaches AI setup — installing Claude Cowork, installing skills, showing the exercise flow. Non-technical.
- **Bonus course:** "Claude Cowork for Personal Decisions" (standalone, not part of main 3-act structure).

**The skill flow per exercise (updated 2026-04-06):**
1. Watch the practical class (30-40 minutes) — learn the concept, see the skill demo
2. Run the module's skill in Claude Cowork (e.g., `/state-map`, `/constraint-id`, `/decision`)
3. Skill asks deep questions — student answers in their own words (thinking-first)
4. Skill saves raw answers to `raw.md` in personal folder
5. Skill generates structured `document.md` for that phase (structuring-second)
6. Skill shows key insights in-chat, points to full document for details

**Output:** By Module 9, the user's personal folder contains: discomfort mapping, state map, target state, constraint ID, decision statement, one-page plan, execution protocol, weekly reviews, win story, and loop closure. A complete decision archive.

**What it does NOT include:** No API. No automation. No dashboard. No live coaching. No traditional community (Wins Board for celebrating victories planned for V2). The user's workspace is their own computer, their own Claude. We provide the methodology and the skills. They own their data.

### Product B: Business Decisions ($1,997/year)

**What it is:** A course that teaches the decision methodology applied to building a business + AI skills + automation APIs that handle distribution, content pipeline, and growth.

**Delivery mechanism:**
- **Course:** Business-specific methodology. Same decision cycle, applied to business decisions: business model, manifesto, methodology, course design, distribution, automation. Each step has its own skill.
- **Skills:** Exercise skills (same as Life Decisions, applied to business) PLUS automation skills that call our APIs (posting, content generation, analytics).
- **APIs:** Automation endpoints for the content pipeline — podcast → clips → multi-platform posting, scheduled content, analytics tracking.
- **Bonus course:** "Vibe Coding for Non-Tech Creators/Founders" (Claude Code course — teaches enough technical fluency to run an AI-native business).

**What the client gets:**
- Course + distribution platform (hosted, ready to use)
- Content automation pipeline (from doc #8 — podcast → clips → multi-platform posting)
- Their own `decisions/` folder structure (modeled after ours)
- Our strategy documents as example/template content
- Access to automation APIs via Claude skills

**Agent-first architecture:**
- APIs + Claude skills compose the platform
- Users interact via Claude Cowork (non-tech) or Claude Code (tech)
- No traditional SaaS dashboard required for core workflow
- We don't compete with Claude — we provide methodology + skills + APIs that work WITH Claude
- Users only touch `decisions/` and `content/` folders — everything else is abstracted

**What Business Decisions is NOT:** Not a no-code builder. Not a marketing agency. Not done-for-you services. We teach the methodology AND give the tools. They do the work.

## 7. The Flywheel

### The compounding engine

```
┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                                                    │
│   CONTENT (social media, 13 accounts)              │
│   ↓                                                │
│   FREE COURSE (email capture + simplified method)  │
│   ↓                                                │
│   LIFE DECISIONS ($197/yr)                         │
│   └── Personal transformation → testimonials       │
│   └── "Now build something" → cross-sell           │
│   ↓                                                │
│   BUSINESS DECISIONS ($1,997/yr)                   │
│   └── Client builds SAME funnel for THEIR audience │
│   └── Uses OUR platform to run THEIR business      │
│   └── Their success = our proof → more content     │
│                                                    │
│   → CYCLE REPEATS                                  │
│                                                    │
└────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Why it compounds

1. **Content feeds acquisition.** Every social post drives to the free course.
2. **Free course feeds conversion.** The methodology works in simplified form — the upgrade is for AI skills and depth.
3. **Life Decisions customers create proof.** Their results become testimonials and content.
4. **Business Decisions clients multiply the system.** They build their own free course → paid course funnel using our platform. Their success validates our methodology.
5. **Our docs ARE the course.** Zero extra content creation for B2B. Our `decisions/` folder is the example.
6. **Dogfooding compounds.** Every improvement to our internal tools improves the B2B product.

### The transparency advantage

Sharing our strategy documents with Business Decisions clients is not a vulnerability. It's a feature:
- **Proof the system works:** "Here's exactly how we built our company using this methodology"
- **Course content:** "Follow along with our actual documents"
- **Trust:** Radical transparency is on-brand (anti-self-help = anti-secrets)
- **Zero extra work:** The docs already exist
