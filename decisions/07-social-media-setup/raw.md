But the personal accounts we start with our own recordings but we develop our AI-strategy so that we have in the future our own voice, our own AI character and everything becomes AI-first. When creating new faceless accounts, they will be 100% faceless. We may manually post in every account if we want to. So the division is not the content itself, is more about the process AND the ICP. Like indy talks to womans and she may have extra subjects she wants to talk about and I talk to men and I will talk about business content in the future so that rightdecision can also help people to make money in the internet. The business itself will be less flexible and the content will be AI-generated to match exactly a lesson, or an act, or a mini-VSL about a specific concept or angle, etc..."

- Future: faceless "dark channels" will be 100% AI-generated

- Division is by PROCESS (AI vs human) AND ICP (Indy → women, Henry → men/business)
- Henry plans to expand rightdecision to also cover "making money on the internet" for men
- Brand content = tightly mapped to methodology (lessons, acts, mini-VSLs, angles)
- Personal content = more flexible, personality-driven, ICP-specific topics
