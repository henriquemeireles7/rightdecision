## Operational Principles

- **Content is distribution.** Blog, social, Substack — all funnel to the product.
- **Document everything.** Strategy docs are the operating system of the company.
- **AI does the work.** Skills, automation, content pipeline — minimize human bottlenecks.
- **One person, not ten.** The company is designed for a solo founder with AI agents.

## Key Strategic Decisions (locked)

- Cross-tenant anonymized data rights in BD ToS from day 1
- Stripe Connect in Phase 2, BaaS in Phase 4+ (after fundraise)
- Decision Protocol: build 3 reference implementations before expecting adoption
- No paid ads until organic demand proves product-market fit
