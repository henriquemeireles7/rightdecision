# Roadmap — Phase 5: Own Hardware (Month 48 → Month 60+)

> Thesis: We control the full stack. Our model runs on our hardware. Decision support is instant, private, and everywhere.
> Flywheel stage: Full autonomous flywheel, model improves continuously from usage
> Revenue target: $50M+ ARR
> Data target: 1B+ decision records

---

## P5.1: On-Device Model + Edge Deployment
**Track:** Runtime Layer

**What:** Model compression for mobile (quantization, distillation). Offline-capable decision support. Privacy-first architecture (decisions never leave the device). Federated learning (model improves without centralizing data). Voice-first interface. Wearable integration.

**Why in the 5-year vision:** "Your decisions never leave your device" is the ultimate trust proposition. On-device means < 100ms inference latency. Federated learning means the model gets smarter from every user without any user's data leaving their phone. This is Decision OS as a true operating system, always on, always private, always fast.

---

## P5.2: Custom Silicon (HORIZON BET)
**Track:** Runtime Layer (REQUIRES $20M+ FUNDRAISE + DEDICATED HARDWARE TEAM)

**What:** ASIC feasibility study. Form factor exploration (standalone device, module, chip). Hardware team hiring. ASIC design for decision model inference. Manufacturing partnerships. Pilot production.

**Why in the 5-year vision:** Full vertical integration. The model runs on silicon designed specifically for decision inference. Maximum performance per watt. This is the endgame of "owning the full stack." Preconditions: own model proven superior (Phase 4), inference cost justifies custom silicon, fundraise complete.

---

## P5.3: Decision Ecosystem + Global Scale
**Track:** All Layers

**What:** Decision OS as a platform (cross-device sync, ambient decision support, life dashboard). Global scale: multi-language, regional adaptation, localized content, government partnerships, NGO partnerships. Full BaaS (debit/credit cards for platform clients). Healthcare-adjacent integration (decision support, not treatment). Education integration. Financial integration.

**Why in the 5-year vision:** This is the cathedral. The Decision OS is the default infrastructure for human decision-making worldwide. Every major life and business decision runs through the system. The network intelligence is unparalleled. The model is the best in the world at understanding how humans decide. And it runs on your hardware.
