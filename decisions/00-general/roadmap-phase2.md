# Roadmap — Phase 2: AI Platform + White-Label (Month 12 → Month 24)

> Thesis: The platform uses AI deeply to improve decision quality. White-label BD scales distribution. Data collection becomes systematic.
> Flywheel stage: User decides → Structured data captured (systematic) → Network patterns emerge
> Revenue target: $500K ARR (conservative: $200K if organic-only)
> Data target: 100K+ structured decision records

## Prerequisites from Phase 1
- Course renders beautifully (P1.1) and people complete it
- Decision Graph Schema V1 validated with real data (P1.2)
- Free-to-paid funnel converting > 5% (P1.3)
- Decision Records being created with follow-up completions > 60% (P1.4)
- 250+ paying LD customers, 3+ paying BD clients
- Legal/consent framework complete (P1.11)
- Social posting provider connected to real platforms (P1.6)

## What Phase 1 Leaves Us With
- Decision Graph with 1,000+ nodes from real users (decisions, outcomes, connections)
- Wins Board with organic social proof
- BD pipeline running real content to real platforms
- Blog + concept pages ranking for target keywords
- Admin dashboard showing key metrics

## P2.1: Intelligent Decision Engine
**Track:** Life Decisions
**Builds on:** Decision Graph (P1.2), Decision Records (P1.4), course progress data

**What:** Decision pattern recognition across users ("people in your situation usually..."). Decision quality scoring based on follow-through data. Personalized methodology paths (skip modules you don't need based on your decision profile). AI-powered decision decomposition (one big decision → actionable sub-decisions). Decision timeline view (your decisions over time, outcomes tracked).

**Why in the 5-year vision:** This is where the Decision OS starts to feel intelligent. The first product that couldn't exist without the Decision Graph. Every interaction teaches what "good decision support" looks like, training data for the own model in Phase 4.

---

## P2.2: Decision Twin (RAG Over Your Decision History)
**Track:** Life Decisions
**Builds on:** Decision Graph, Claude API, existing skills (decide, target-state)

**What:** A persistent AI context that retrieves your full decision history, patterns, and outcomes via RAG. Not a fine-tuned persona. A retrieval system that makes Claude (or future own model) aware of your complete decision graph. When you start a new decision, the Twin says: "Last time you faced a similar choice (leaving a job in 2024), you took 3 weeks to act and rated the outcome 8/10. What's different this time?"

**Why in the 5-year vision:** The Decision Twin is the single most compelling feature for retention. It's the AI that actually KNOWS you, not from a chat history, but from structured decision data with real outcomes. This is what makes the own model in Phase 4 possible: teaching AI to be a genuine decision partner.

---

## P2.3: Collective Decision Intelligence (Network Activation)
**Track:** Life Decisions (Social Layer → Intelligence Layer)
**Builds on:** Wins Board (already has 4 life areas, anonymous timestamps), Decision Graph

**What:** Activate network intelligence at density thresholds per decision category. Pattern detection across wins. Decision archetype matching ("your decision pattern is similar to..."). Win prediction based on decision type + follow-through patterns. Seasonal patterns. Outcome tracking with longitudinal data. Anonymous Decision Twin (peer matching). Cascade Visualization (see how decisions interconnect in your graph).

**Why in the 5-year vision:** THE MOAT. "73% of people who decided X and acted within 7 days reported positive outcomes at 90 days." That evidence comes from the network. No competitor can replicate it. This is what the own model trains on.

## P2.4: Self-Serve White-Label BD Platform
**Track:** Business Decisions
**Builds on:** Full 7-step pipeline (working), workflow orchestrator, existing BD infrastructure

**What:** Transform the BD pipeline from "Henry sets it up for you" to "sign up, configure, and your content pipeline runs automatically." Client isolation (each client gets their own data silo). White-label branding. Client dashboard (pipeline stats, audience growth, revenue). Stripe Connect integration (X% platform cut on every client transaction). Onboarding automation (new client → fully set up in < 1 hour).

**Why in the 5-year vision:** Every BD client is a distribution channel. Every BD client's users generate decision data for the network (cross-tenant data rights in ToS). Stripe Connect creates the financial layer. This is the Shopify moment.

## P2.5: Mobile App
**Track:** Life Decisions
**Builds on:** All existing APIs (course, wins, progress, decision records)

**What:** Decision capture on the go (voice → structured decision record). Daily decision prompt (morning notification). Quick wins logging. Offline support. Home screen widget ("Today's commitment"). Push notifications for follow-up loops.

**Why in the 5-year vision:** Decisions happen in real life, not at a desk. Mobile expands TAM and decision capture frequency. More decisions = faster flywheel. The app is also the future home for the on-device model in Phase 5.

---

## P2.6: AI Content Intelligence
**Track:** Business Decisions
**Builds on:** analytics-collect step (engagement data), post-distribute step, insight-generate step

**What:** Upgrade the existing insight-generate step from basic AI recommendations to predictive content intelligence. Content performance prediction (before posting, estimate engagement). Audience analysis (who engages, when, why). Content strategy AI (recommend what to create next). Cross-platform optimization. Viral potential scoring.

**Why in the 5-year vision:** BD clients need to see ROI. Content intelligence shows them what's working. The AI content engine becomes a competitive moat for the white-label platform.

---

## P2.7: Vibe Coding Course for BD Clients
**Track:** Business Decisions
**Builds on:** The platform itself, existing course infrastructure (course hub, progress, access gating)

**What:** "Building with AI for Non-Tech Creators" course. 6 modules teaching BD clients to understand and customize their white-label platform. Framed as "building with AI" not "building with Claude" (future-proof for own model). Hands-on exercises using the actual BD platform.

**Why in the 5-year vision:** Non-tech BD clients who understand their platform are stickier, more successful, and generate more decision data. Course serves double duty as BD marketing content.

---

## P2.8: AI Course Tutor
**Track:** Life Decisions
**Builds on:** Course hub, existing Claude skills (decide, target-state), Decision Records

**What:** Adaptive course pacing (AI detects comprehension level). Socratic questioning (AI pushes back on weak decisions). Real-time exercise feedback. Spaced repetition for methodology concepts. Practice decisions (low-stakes to build the deciding muscle). Decision journaling with AI analysis.

**Why in the 5-year vision:** The tutor makes the course dramatically more effective, improving outcomes → improving Collective Intelligence data quality → making the own model better. The tutor IS the prototype for what the own model should be good at.
