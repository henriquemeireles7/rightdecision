# Company — Right Decision

> Last verified: 2026-06-15

## Mission

**Help people live a more fulfilled and longer life.**

That is the whole of the product vision this repository commits to. Everything more specific —
positioning, ICP, pricing, the course narrative, the methodology, the feature roadmap — lives
OUTSIDE the repo because it changes with customer feedback. None of it should ever block or
override a technical decision. (See the decoupling rule in the root `CLAUDE.md`.)

## Who we build for

The avatar is the founder from a few years ago: someone who has "done the work" and now wants a
more integrated, whole life — not another framework to consume, but a place to get what they
want onto paper and then live it. This is a description of the user we serve, not marketing copy.

## What we are

Right Decision is an infobusiness + software company. US-registered LLC. Solo developer (Henry)
+ AI agents + content partner (Indy). The product is a software **handbook**: the member writes
what they want, turns it into a plan, and builds it into a routine. A paid yearly membership
includes the course that teaches them how to use it. We sell it as an infobusiness with software,
not as a self-serve SaaS.

## What we are NOT

- Not a therapy replacement or mental-health service
- Not a coaching practice (no 1-on-1 sessions, no dependency)
- Not a generic SaaS tool sold by the seat
- Not competing with Claude/AI tools — we build ON them

## Operating principles

> These are how the COMPANY and its harness operate. They are intentionally product-agnostic.
> See decisions/maturity.md for the maturity operating system and "Applied" examples.

1. **Agent-as-execution** — Agents do the work. Humans set direction and make taste decisions.
2. **One-person-company** — Solo founder + AI agents. No employees until agents can't do the job.
3. **Strategy-before-code** — Every piece of code traces back to an intent; the repo stays a clean
   technology surface, not a strategy archive.
4. **Zero recurring errors** — Every failure produces a prevention artifact. The system gets smarter.
5. **Taste is the moat** — AI commoditizes execution. Founder judgment is the real defensibility.
6. **Boil the lake, flag the ocean** — Always do the complete thing when the marginal cost is minutes.
7. **Ship the learning, not the feature** — Optimize for learning velocity, not feature completeness.
8. **Documentation compounds** — Good docs are reusable assets; stale product vision in code is a liability.

## Where product strategy lives

Detailed, evolving product strategy (the four handbook pillars, course structure, pricing,
positioning) is maintained outside this repository so it can change freely without churning the
codebase. Historical strategy documents are preserved under `decisions/*/00-legacy/` for
reference only — they describe a previous direction and are NOT authoritative.
