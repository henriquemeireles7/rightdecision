# Clips Rules

## Purpose
Short video/audio clips extracted from longer content (podcast episodes, course recordings,
webinars). These are NOT scripts — they are selection + editing guides.

## Clip Types
- **Insight clip** (15-60s) — One powerful statement or realization
- **Story clip** (60-120s) — A complete mini-story with beginning, middle, insight
- **Teaching clip** (30-90s) — One concept explained simply
- **Reaction clip** (15-30s) — A surprising or emotional moment

## Selection Criteria
When selecting clips from source material:
1. **Emotional peak** — The moment where energy shifts (surprise, laughter, realization)
2. **Quotable line** — A sentence that works standalone as text overlay
3. **Counter-intuitive** — Anything that challenges assumptions
4. **Story arc** — A complete micro-story (setup → tension → resolution)
5. **Specificity** — Clips with specific numbers, names, or moments > vague wisdom

## Output Format
```markdown
## Clip: {title}

- **Source:** {episode/recording name}
- **Timestamp:** {start} - {end}
- **Duration:** {seconds}s
- **Type:** insight | story | teaching | reaction
- **Hook line:** "{the first thing the viewer hears}"
- **Quotable:** "{text overlay candidate}"
- **Caption:** {social media caption}
- **Platform:** reels | tiktok | youtube-shorts | linkedin
- **Hashtags:** {5-10 relevant hashtags}
```

## Quality Checklist
- [ ] Clip works without context (viewer hasn't seen the full episode)
- [ ] First 3 seconds hook the viewer
- [ ] Has a clear quotable for text overlay
- [ ] Ends on a strong note (not trailing off)
- [ ] Caption follows social rules (rules-social.md)
- [ ] Under 90 seconds (60s optimal for reels/tiktok)
